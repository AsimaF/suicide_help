Suicide Prevention


This website is designed to help bring awareness and give advice on suicide prevention


If you require urgent help please call Samaritans UK on 116 123


If you have seriously harmed yourself - for example, by taking a drug overdose - or you feel your may be about to harm yourself, call 999 for an ambulance or go straight to A&E. Or ask someone else to call 999 or take you to A&E.
