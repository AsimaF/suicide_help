Suicide Crisis Lines


If you're feeling like you want to die, it's important to tell someone. Help and support is available right now if you need it.

You do not have to struggle with difficult feelings alone


Phone a Helpline

Samaritans - for everyone
Call 116 123
Email jo@samaritans.org

Against Living Miserably (CALM)
Call 0800 58 58 58

Paypyrus - for people under 35
Call 0800 068 41 41
Text 07860039967
Email pat@papyrus-uk.org

Childline - for children and young people under 19
Call 0800 1111 - this number will not show up on your phone bill

Silence of Suicide (SOS) - for everyone
Call 0300 1020 505 - 4pm to mightnight every day
Email support@sossilenceofsuicide.org


Message a 24/7 text line

Shout Crisis Text Line - for everyone
Text "SHOUT" to 85258

YoungMinds Crisis Messenger - for people under 19
Text "YM" to 85258
